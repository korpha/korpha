Social posting

Each business line has its own saved browser profile per platform — different brand voices, different accounts, completely separate sessions. Click Log in on a row to open a real browser window, sign in once with the right brand account, then close it. Future posts from that line reuse the saved session.

No API keys, no scrapers, no ToS-violating adapters: it's literally your logged-in browser, driven by the agent only when you tell it to.

CLI parity

Everything on this page mirrors korpha social:

  • korpha social status — same table per business line
  • korpha social login <slug> --unit <line-slug> — opens the login window for that line
  • korpha social post <slug> --unit <line-slug> --text "..." — drives the post
  • korpha social post <slug> --unit <line> --file post.md --dry-run — preview the goal without opening a browser
